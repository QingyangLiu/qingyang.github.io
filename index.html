Qingyang Liu, David G. Shatwell, Parth Parag Kulkarni, Mubarak Shah · Under Review

Introduces SkyReg, a geometry-aware drone-to-satellite geo-registration method together with SkyReg-130k and SkyReg-Bench for dense pixel-wise geographic alignment.

Proposes the Semantic Drift Protocol for unified image-text models and evaluates how well semantic meaning is preserved across repeated image-to-text and text-to-image generations.

About

I am a computer vision researcher currently pursuing a PhD at the Center for Research in Computer Vision at the University of Central Florida under Dr. Mubarak Shah. My recent work focuses on cross-view understanding, geolocalization, and geo-registration, especially for drone and satellite imagery.

Before starting the PhD, I completed a BS in Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin-Madison and an MS in Computer Science at Florida State University. My background spans research, teaching, and product-oriented development, including work in machine learning, mobile systems, databases, and applied AI.
